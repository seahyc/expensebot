# Glints Expense Policy (effective 1 Mar 2026)

## Submission rules
- Platform: OmniHR at glints.omnihr.co
- Foreign currency: convert using Google Live Rate or credit card rate
- Receipt deadline: within 60 days of transaction
- Payout: cut-off 15th each month, paid with payroll

## Valid receipts
- Official receipts / VAT invoices showing amount, date, product/service
- Must be dated within 60 days of transaction

## Invalid receipts
- Bank transfer or e-wallet screenshots
- Unclear / unreadable photos
- Receipts outside the same calendar year
- Unrelated receipts

## Claim categories

### 1. Flexible Benefits
- Subs: Physical, Family, Mental, Career and Work
- Account: 30381 Flexi-benefits
- Approver: People Ops
- Budget cap: YES | Eligibility: FTEs only, 80% co-payment

### 2. Laptop Maintenance
- Subs: Laptop Repair, Hardware Support
- Account: 30383 Productivity
- Approver: People Ops
- Budget cap: YES

### 3. Medical Claims
- Account: 30382 Medical benefits
- Approver: People Ops
- Budget cap: YES | Eligibility: Remote, TW, CN employees only

### 4. International Travel
- Subs: Flight/Train/Ferry Tickets, Transportation, Entry Visa & Travel Taxes, Accommodations, SIM/Telco Services, Travel Insurance, Daily Meal Allowance
- Account: 30560 Travel-International
- Approver: Department Leader (Daily Meal: People Ops)
- Budget cap: NO (except Daily Meal Allowance)
- Transport: normal class PHV (Grab, Gojek) or taxi only

### 5. Business Expense
- Subs:
  - Call Packages — 30570 Telephone & Internet (Sales only)
  - Courier & Postage
  - Team Reward — 30730 Employee Engagement
  - Local Travel — 30550 Travel-Local (Sales only)
  - Client Meetings & Entertainment — 30520 Entertainment
- Approver: Manager + Department Leader
- Client entertainment must be infrequent, not lavish, necessary for business

### 6. General Affairs
- Subs: Office Supplies (30510), Courier & Postage (30510), Local Travel (30550)
- Approver: People Team
- Budget cap: NO | Eligibility: GA PIC only

### 7. Team Bonding
- Account: 30730 Employee Engagement
- Approver: Department Leader
- Budget cap: NO | Per 2026 Team Bonding Budget Policy

## Not claimable (direct vendor payment only)
Legal/Professional Charges, Rent & Utilities, Bank & Credit Card Charges, Web Hosting & Security, Subscriptions, Marketing

## Non-reimbursable
- Gifts/meals/travel to government officials
- Travel expenses of spouse or family on business trips
- Expenses paid for customers' or business partners' travel
